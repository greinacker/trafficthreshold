Traffic Threshold Pepper

This pepper sends an alert via email when your site hits a certain number of
page views per minute.

Note that access to certain APIs is required for this to work; when you install
the pepper it will test to ensure it will be able to work, and show you a
message if not.

Installing Pepper
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Upload the /gregreinacker/ directory and its contents to 
/mint/pepper/.

2. Login to your Mint installation and in the Preferences click "Install" under
Pepper.

3. Click the Traffic Threshold Pepper "Install" button. Click "Okay."

4. Fill in the preferences as desired, and click "Done."
